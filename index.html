Fae Academy: Trials of the Chosen

Lyra Nightshade has spent nineteen years believing she possesses no magic whatsoever. When she receives a mysterious acceptance letter to Thornwood Academy—the most prestigious magical institution in the realm—she's certain it's a mistake.

At Thornwood, ancient shadows stir beneath purple-tinged skies. The Veil between realms weakens, and Lyra discovers abilities she never knew she possessed—powers connected to the very forces threatening to break through.

When Lyra discovers her mother isn't dead but trapped in the Void for the past twenty years, she has mere weeks to master her newfound abilities and execute a daring rescue that could either save both realms or destroy the boundary between them forever.

About Lyra

Lyra Blackwood is a passionate creator of romantasy fiction who believes in the transformative power of both magic and love. Her stories blend elements of fantasy, romance, and adventure, creating immersive worlds where readers can escape reality.

With a background in mythology and folklore, Lyra weaves ancient tales and modern sensibilities into her narratives, creating stories that feel both timeless and fresh. Her debut novel, "Fae Academy: Trials of the Chosen," introduces readers to the world of Thornwood Academy and the five fae courts.

When not crafting tales of fae courts and forbidden romance, Lyra can be found exploring ancient forests, collecting crystals, and drinking entirely too much tea. She lives with her familiar (a black cat named Shadow) and is currently working on the next book in the Fae Academy saga.
